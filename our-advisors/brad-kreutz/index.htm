As the Partner and Head of Lower Middle Market Advisory at INTERDOT Advisors, Brad Kreutz specializes in the transformation of businesses within the $10M-$100M+ revenue range, elevating them into industry-leading enterprises. With over two decades of entrepreneurial experience and an impressive record of over 7,000 hours of world-class advisory, he possesses a deep understanding of the daily challenges that weigh on CEOs, founders, and business owners. 

Brad partners with ambitious owners to elevate both their businesses and teams. Using his insights as a 4x founder, he provides both strategic guidance and tactical steps to scale your business effectively.

Brad’s track record of exceptional results speaks for itself:

  • He catapulted a law firm’s revenue by an impressive 450% in just five years.
  • Brad achieved a remarkable 3X increase in revenue and project averages for a recruitment client, surpassing their 5-year goal in 18 months.
  • Brad successfully scaled a property management franchise system, enhancing KPIs across locations and facilitating a franchisee exit at a premium multiple.

Brad excels in developing Comprehensive Business Engines fine-tuning critical aspects of business operations, including marketing, sales, recruiting, leadership, financials, and productivity. He creates specialized engines for each department, ensuring that every facet of the business runs smoothly. His primary focus centers on fostering leadership teams and aligning their efforts with the strategic objectives of the organization. Brad tailors his solutions to suit each business’s unique needs and scale, resulting in seamless operational efficiency. 

As a INTERDOT Advisor, Brad Kreutz brings a wealth of experience and expertise. With a focus on strategy and implementation, he adopts a hands-on, holistic approach, using proven tools and methodologies to enhance enterprises and exit value. His tailored advisory solutions address the unique challenges each business faces, driving meaningful changes that impact the bottom line. Brad’s Lower Middle Market (LMM) practice offers the choice of a Partner Advisor or a dedicated Advisory Team from their extensive 50+ Advisor Team. He is renowned for consistently delivering double-digit revenue and profit growth and maintaining a world-class Net Promoter Score (NPS).

Beyond his business acumen, Brad leads a fulfilling life in Denver with his wife Julie and their sons Leopold and August. He enjoys skiing, hiking, and cheering on Chicago sports teams. Besides his deep knowledge of t-shirts, cigars, and the world of business, Brad once found happiness as a Space Camper.
